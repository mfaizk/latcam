**Full Description (Max 4000 chars):**  
LatCam is the ultimate professional camera application designed for explorers, real estate agents, field workers, and photography enthusiasts who want context tied directly to their photos. No more guessing where or when a picture was taken!

With LatCam, every photo you capture is beautifully and accurately stamped with your current location coordinates, the local address, and the exact date and time. Combining a sleek, premium dark-mode interface with blazing-fast hardware-level camera controls, capturing context safely and beautifully has never been easier.

Key Features:
📍 Instant GPS Watermarking – Automatically overlays Latitude, Longitude, and formatted address lines directly onto your image.
🕒 Timestamps – Ensures indisputable proof of when a photo was taken with precise real-time date and time stamps.
⚙️ Fully Customizable – A beautiful bottom-sheet settings menu lets you independently toggle Latitude, Longitude, Address, Time, and Date, giving you total control over what data is presented. 
🔎 Advanced Vision Camera – Powered by incredibly fast, low-level native camera functionality. Enjoy crystal-clear image quality with fluid pinch-to-zoom support.
💾 Automated Organization – Photos are cleanly saved in your device's Media Library retaining GPS EXIF Data alongside the direct visible watermark.
📱 Modern Premium UI – Enjoy a sleek 'glassmorphism' user interface utilizing custom aesthetic colors and micro-animations instead of basic plain menus. Fluid, fast, and easy on the eyes.

Whether you're cataloging construction progress, keeping a travel diary, mapping out new territories, or taking professional reference photos... LatCam adds the vital "Where and When" to your photos without requiring extra effort.

Download LatCam today and give your photos the context they deserve!
