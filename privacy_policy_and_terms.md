## 1. Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** April 5, 2026

**1. Introduction**
Welcome to LatCam ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and handle your information when you use the LatCam mobile application ("App").

**2. Information We Collect**
LatCam is designed to function entirely entirely on-device, prioritizing your privacy. We request access to the following hardware and software features:
- **Camera:** Required to capture photos and provide real-time viewfinder previews.
- **Location Services (GPS):** Optional but recommended. If granted, your latitude, longitude, and formatted address are accessed *locally* to embed as visual watermarks and EXIF metadata within the photos you take.
- **Media Library / Storage:** Required to save the photos you capture to your device's photo gallery and to read them back for the in-app gallery preview.

**3. How We Use Your Information**
We do **not** collect, store, transmit, or share your photos, location data, or any other personal information on any external servers. All operations, including image processing and location watermarking, occur strictly locally on your physical device. The EXIF data and visual stamps are embedded directly into the image files saved on your device.

**4. Data Sharing and Disclosure**
Because LatCam does not collect or transmit your data to remote servers, we have no user data to share, sell, or disclose to third parties, advertisers, or analytics providers.

**5. Permissions Management**
You retain full control over the permissions granted to LatCam. You can revoke Camera, Location, or Storage access at any time via your device's Settings menu. Note that revoking Camera or Storage access will prevent the core functionality of the App.

**6. Changes to This Privacy Policy**
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the App or on our App Store listing. Changes are effective immediately upon publishing.
